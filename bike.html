Introduce

A motorbike (also called a motorcycle) is a two-wheeled vehicle powered by an engine. It is one of the most popular forms of transport around the world, especially in busy cities and developing countries.

Special

Fast and convenient in heavy traffic.

Fuel-efficient, cheaper to run than cars

Easy to park and takes up less space.

Popular for both daily commuting and recreational riding.
